The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the south bank of the Yamuna river in the Indian city of Agra, India. In 2007, it was declared a winnero f the New 7 Wonders of the World(2000- 2007) initiative

Fast Facts
Year of Construction: 1631
Completed In: 1653
Time Taken: 22 years
Built By: Shah Jahan
Dedicated to: Mumtaz Mahal, the wife of Shah Jahan
Cost of Construction: 32 crore rupees
Number of workers: 20,000
