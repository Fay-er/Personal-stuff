Basic Mathematics Formulae


These are some of the most commonly used formulae in Mathematics


Algebra

  1. a2 - b2 = (a + b)(a - b)
  2. (a + b)2 = a2 + b2 + 2ab
  3. (a - b)2 = a2 + b2 - 2ab

Mensuration

  1. Area of a Square = l2(Where l is the length of a side of a square)
  2. Area of a Rectangle = l x b (Where l and b are the length and the breadth of the rectangle, respectively)
  3. Area of a Circle = Pi x r2 (Where Pi is a constaant whose value is taken as 22/7 or 3.14 and r represents the radius of the circle)
